New Book by Tom Fowler

We're excited to let you know that Tom Fowler's book, Carolina Journeys: Exploring the Trails of the Carolina's — Both Real and Imagined is now available in paperback. Like the stories on this website, the 34 essays in this book introduce us to little known sites of interest in the Carolinas.

Carolina Journeys speaks of history, culture and specific places — we also hope that these stories will inspire our readers to perform their own weekend explorations to discover or rediscover the different trails, real and imagined, that can be found throughout the Carolinas.

Winner of the 2004 Willie Parker Peace History Book Award of the North Carolina Society of History, the judges commented, "What a remarkable book! What a wonderful style!" Reviewer Carol Volk writes that Carolina Journeys is "written as an inspiration to help the reader discover his or her own unique adventure, and is a treat for maverick tourists and armchair travelers alike."

You can buy this book from your local bookstore, from the online booksellers, or directly from the publisher, Parkway Publishers, Inc., P.O. Box 28607, Boone, NC 28607 www.parkwaypublishers.com.

Ask for Carolina Journeys by Tom Fowler (ISBN: 1-887905-86-3), $14.95 paperback.

For additional information contact CarolinaJourneys' webmistress, Marjorie Fowler.
