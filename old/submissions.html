SUBMISSIONS

CarolinaJourneys.com invites the submission of unsolicited, original articles about trails, travels, travails, and places in North and South Carolina. Submissions must be made by regular mail. Email submissions will not be considered. Please include (a) any illustrations, maps, or photographs (with captions) that should accompany your article, (b) related links for those interested in your topic, and (c) a brief biographical sketch about yourself and how we can contact you. Publishing and editorial decisions are based on the subject matter of the article and the editors' judgment of the quality of the writing, and the potential interest to the readers of CarolinaJourneys.com.

Should we accept your article we will ask you to provide us with an electronic version. Manuscripts will not be returned unless you include a self-addressed, sufficiently stamped envelope. Please include a single stamped envelope (SASE) if you would prefer we send a response only. Mail your submissions to: Marjorie Fowler, 1417 Morris Road, Pittsboro, N.C. 27312.

Thank you for your interest in CarolinaJourneys and we look forward to receiving your submissions.
